# Tanur — Brand & Design System

Premium, institutional, calm — modelled on ondo.finance. Clean white/black with a
**nickel steel-blue** accent and a **molten-ember** secondary (the *tanur*/smelter
metaphor: raw ore → value), rounded-geometric type, generous whitespace.

## Palette
- Backgrounds: near-white `#FBFBFC` (`bg`), light grey `#F4F4F6` (`bg-2`), white cards
- Lines: `#EAEAEC` / `#E0E0E4`
- Text: near-black `#0B0B0C` (`ink`), muted `#5B5D66`, faint `#9A9CA4`
- Accent (nickel steel-blue): `#2E6F8E` (`brand`), mid `#3C86A8`, bright `#4FA3C7`, tint `#E6F0F5`
- Graphic accents: molten ember `#E2742E` (smelter heat), periwinkle `#7C7FE8`
- Primary buttons + floating nav = **black** pill; steel-blue for links/eyebrows.

## Typography
- Display + body: geometric grotesque (Space Grotesk stand-in).
- Data / hashes: **JetBrains Mono**, tabular-nums.

## Motion
framer-motion + lenis. Scroll-morph hero, count-up numbers, whileInView reveals,
alternating light/dark sections, giant wordmark.

## Voice
Honest RWA. The data is the moat: LME + HPM (ESDM) + Antam, on-chain oracle
reputation. AI guards data trust — it is not the headline. Indonesian roots
(Morowali, IMIP, hilirisasi), global reach. Tagline:
*"Own a fractional, yield-bearing claim on Indonesia's nickel revenue."*
